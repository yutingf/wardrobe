Photos and catalog live in this browser only; export the catalog to move it to another device.

Take several angles of each piece, select many photos at once, or paste an image from the clipboard (the button, or Ctrl/Cmd+V anywhere); a photo holding several separated pieces gets split automatically. Saved photos become clean catalog shots: background removed, straightened, on white. On a phone, "Take photo" opens the camera; on a computer it opens the file picker. Everything runs in your browser: photos never leave this device. The first analysis downloads the vision models (~150 MB, cached afterwards).
